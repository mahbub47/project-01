SOS Button

The SOS Button is a critical feature that allows users to send instant distress signals during an emergency. With just one tap, the app alerts pre-configured emergency contacts and nearby volunteers, sharing the user’s exact location. This quick response system ensures that help is on the way, providing peace of mind in high-stress situations where time is crucial, especially when other communication methods may fail.

Emergency Contact Feature

The Emergency Contact feature allows users to store and manage important contact information for immediate access during a disaster. Users can input emergency contacts, including family members, friends, medical professionals, and nearby volunteers. In the event of an emergency, the app enables users to quickly send distress messages or alerts with their current location to these contacts, ensuring help is reached without delay. This feature is crucial for maintaining communication during disasters when phone networks may be unstable, providing users with a reliable way to notify others and seek assistance.

Disaster Preparedness Guide

The Disaster Preparedness Guide provides users with essential information to prepare for and respond to various disasters. It includes detailed evacuation plans, first-aid tutorials, checklists for emergency supplies, and region-specific disaster training. Tailored to the needs of local communities, this feature ensures users are well-equipped to handle emergencies, offering life-saving advice in multiple languages for maximum accessibility.
